About the Author – Jonaxx

Who is Jonaxx?

Jonahmae Panen Pacala, known to millions as Jonaxx, is a legendary Filipino author best known for her emotionally gripping and plot-twisting stories. She started writing at the age of 9 and hasn’t stopped since. With her powerful narratives, unforgettable characters, and raw portrayal of love and pain, she has earned her place as the Pop Fiction Queen and one of the most followed authors worldwide on Wattpad.

Writing Journey

Started writing at age: 9; online at age 12 (Creative Corner)

First story: Shakespeare in Love (written at age 12)

First published book: Mapapansin Kaya (with multiple reprints)

First self-published book: 24 Signs of Summer (24SOS)

Exclusive publisher: Only author under Mpress, her exclusive publishing imprint under Summit

2013: Writer of the Year + Best Story of the Year (Baka Sakali)

2014: Gawad Kasulatan Awardee

2015: Won Filipino Readers’ Choice Award (Heartless)

Milestone: First Wattpad author to hold a booksigning in an arena (Baka Sakali Trilogy)
