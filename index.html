About Jonaxx

Jonaxx
Jonahmae Panen Pacala, popularly known as Jonaxx, is one of the most beloved Filipino authors in the digital age. Her emotionally intense and gripping stories on Wattpad and PopFiction have captured millions of hearts worldwide.
With her raw and powerful storytelling, she continues to inspire readers through unforgettable characters and compelling narratives that resonate deeply with her audience.
